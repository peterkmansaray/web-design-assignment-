About Football Agency Sierra Leone

We are a small, focused team dedicated to giving young Sierra Leonean footballers professional support and honest guidance.

Our Story

Football Agency Sierra Leone was created after seeing many talented players with limited access to structured training, fair representation or basic career advice. We began by organising community sessions and informal mentorship, and have grown into a simple but committed agency.

Today, we combine training, personal development and exposure. We insist on discipline, respect and education, so that players are prepared for life on and off the pitch.

What We Do

  • • Identify promising players through trials and community games.
  • • Provide basic fitness and technical training programmes.
  • • Record match footage and short highlight clips.
  • • Connect players to clubs, scouts and scholarship opportunities.
  • • Guide families on contracts, travel and education decisions.

Our Values

Honesty: Clear, realistic communication with players and families.

Discipline: Respect for time, training and team-mates.

Education: Encouraging players to continue school or skills training.

Opportunity: Opening doors without exploiting young talent.
